ABOUT US

OUR TEAM

Brilliant Youth Technologies company is a youths' company in Kenya with technological skills which enables youths who are still in school to use their skills to work for our nation.

  • Youths are smart, energetic and creative with potential in technological innovations.
  • Youths are cheap and efficient to work without the aim of exploit.
  • Youths are active in group works with great co-ordination with the client.

Mision

To provide university and college students with a platform to use their technological skills to work for local and international clients as they gain more experience for their portfolio.

Vision

To be a internationally recognised platform working for both local and international projects engaging the youths giving them exposure and recognition.
